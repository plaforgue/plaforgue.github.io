Third Person Bio

Pierre Laforgue is a post-doctoral researcher at the University of Milan, working among the LAILA team led by professor Nicolò Cesa-Bianchi. His current research interests revolve around multitask online learning and bandit algorithms, with applications to federated learning. Prior to this, he obtained his PhD from Télécom Paris, where he studied kernel methods and robust statistical learning under the supervision of professors Florence d'Alché-Buc and Stephan Clémençon.
